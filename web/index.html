Fixed control flags around the city. Open the app, pick a course, run it, punch each flag — get your time and your place on the leaderboard.

How it works

  1. Pick a course on the map in the app.
  2. Run — the app records your GPS track with the screen off.
  3. Punch each flag — tap it with your phone (NFC) or scan its QR / type its number.
  4. Get ranked — your run is verified (you were really there, on a real track) and placed on the leaderboard for your class.
